Jollof Rice Recipe

A delicious looking plate of jollof rice

Ingredients

  • Rice (A few cups)
  • Groundnut oil
  • Grounded pepper (Mixture of tomato and red pepper)
  • Salt
  • Seasoning (A few cubes)
  • Curry and Thyme
  • Onions (Sliced)
  • Meat stock (From boiled chicken or beef)
  • Carrots (Diced)
  • Cabbage (Sliced)
  • Green peas

Steps

  1. Add a little amount of groundnut oil in a clean pot on fire
  2. After the oil is heated to an extent, the sliced onions are added and it's allowed to get slightly brown, although this is not necessary
  3. Tomato paste and subsequently, grounded pepper are then added to the mixture. Everything is then fried together
  4. After frying, the required amount of seasoning, salt and meat stock are added. Curry and meat stock are added thereafter. It is then covered and allowed to boil. A little water could also be added if needed.
  5. At this point, washed rice is added and allowed to cook.
  6. Carrots, cabbage and green peas can be added at this point
  7. After the rice has been cooked to the desrired texture, it is hen taken off the fire.
  • Be careful not to add too much water to prevent the rice from being too soft.
  • Also, do not stir the rice to prevent it from being burnt underneath.
