Egusi Soup Recipe

Egusi soup is one that is popular in a lot of tribes in Southern Nigeria, this has caused it to have a lot of ways in which it can be prepared. This recipe, however, is the Yoruba way of preparing it.

Ingredients

  • Melon (Grinded)
  • Palm oil
  • Seasoning and salt
  • Fish
  • Meat
  • Ponmo
  • Grounded pepper (Boiled mixture of blended tomatoes and red pepper)
  • Ugwu (Sliced and thoroughly rinsed)
  • Locust beans
  • Meat stock (Water from boiled chicken and beef)

Steps

  1. A pot is put on fire, palm oil is then added after all the water in the pot is dried.
  2. After the added palm oil has been heated well, the grinded melon and locust beand are added till it is almost dried.
  3. Thereafter, the grounded pepper is added to the partially fried egusi. It is allowed to boil for a while.
  4. After that, the meat stock, fish, meat and ponmo are added.
  5. Seasoning and salt are added to taste.
  6. After everything boils for a while, the sliced ugwu is then added.
  7. You allow it to cook until the water dries off and tada!, your delicious egusi soup is ready to eat.
